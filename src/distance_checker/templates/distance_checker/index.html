This page is to help engineers in their daily tasks.

For now there are available:

Corner Distance

'Corner distance' is a module for checking the minimum distance to the edge of structural connections in the corners of frame structures. The module takes into account factors such as the type of bolts and the thickness of the plate in the connection, verifying whether there is sufficient space for on-site installation.

Ridge Distance

'Ridge distance' is a module for checking the minimum distance to the edge of structural connections in the ridge of frame structures. The module takes into account factors such as the type of bolts and the thickness of the plate in the connection, verifying whether there is sufficient space for on-site installation.

Bracing

'Bracing' is a module that helps calculate and estimate the lightest CFRHS (Circular Formed Rectangular Hollow Sections) with limitations on deflection for various forces.
